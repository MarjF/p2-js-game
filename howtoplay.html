How to play?

  • The game is divided into levels. Each level consists of a number of logos that the player must identify.

  • The player starts each level with three lives. If the player incorrectly identifies a logo, they lose one life.

  • The player earns one coin for each correctly identified logo. Coins can be used to unlock new logos, new levels, or other features in the game life will be going to reset to 3 if you pass the current level or once you got a correct answer.

  • If the player correctly identifies all the logos in a level, their lives are reset to three and they move on to the next level. There is no option to skip the level unless you got the correct answer.

  • The game ends when the player loses all three lives. At the end of the game, the player's final score is displayed.
